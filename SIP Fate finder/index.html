The Evolution of Religion

Religion has shaped humanity since the dawn of civilization. Ancient belief systems like animism, shamanism, and polytheism were among the first spiritual frameworks, offering explanations for natural events and guiding early communities. As societies evolved, so did organized religion. Major world religions such as Hinduism, Judaism, Buddhism, Christianity, and Islam developed rich traditions and texts that continue to influence billions today. From sacred temples to modern digital spirituality, religion remains a powerful force for meaning, connection, and identity in every era.
